FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Here are some common queries from our students.

What courses does Achievers Academy offer?

We offer comprehensive coaching for SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC CPO, SSC Stenographer, Banking, RPSC, RSMSSB, CET, CTET, REET, Defence, GATE, UGC NET, and Civil Services examinations.

Do you provide both online and offline classes?

Yes, we offer both online live interactive classes and offline classroom sessions at our Bikaner center. You can choose the mode that suits your schedule and learning preference.

What are your working hours?

Achievers Academy is open 24 hours, allowing students to study and access facilities at any time that fits their schedule. Our faculty is available during designated teaching hours.

How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by visiting our campus at 4-D-35, JNV Murti Circle, Sector 2, Jay Narayan Vyas Colony, Bikaner, or by calling/WhatsApp at 09521495454. You can also fill the contact form on our website and we'll get back to you.

Do you provide study material and test series?

Absolutely! We provide expertly curated books, study material, regular test series, and doubt-clearing sessions as part of our comprehensive coaching program. Our books are also available for individual purchase.

What is the fee structure for courses?

Fee structure varies depending on the course and duration. We offer competitive pricing with flexible payment options. Please contact us at 09521495454 or visit our center for detailed fee information and current discounts.
